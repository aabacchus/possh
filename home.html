The Standard Model

A short guide



This website aims to give you a brief explanation of the particles in the Standard Model of Particle Physics, an understanding of how these particles are discovered, and also featuring a special article on the Higgs Boson and Field.

Please note the author is not in any way professional, or educated in these matters - he has done simple research on the internet and in various books and this website is a collection of some of his findings.
