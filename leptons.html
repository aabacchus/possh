Leptons

An important group containing the electron and neutrinos

There are 6 leptons, and their 6 antiparticles.




Electron - e-

Mass: 0.511 MeV/c2
Charge: -1
Spin: 1/2
Generation: 1
Year and location of discovery: J.J. Thompson, England, 1897


Electron Neutrino - ve

Mass: eV/c2
Charge: 0
Spin: 1/2
Generation: 1
Year and location of discovery: Clyde Cowan + Frederick Reines, 1956


Muon - μ

Mass: 105.7eV/c2
Charge: -1
Spin: 1/2
Generation: 2
Year and location of discovery: Carl D. Anderson + Seth Neddermeyer, 1936


Muon Neutrino - vμ

Mass: eV/c2
Charge: 0
Spin: 1/2
Generation: 2
Year and location of discovery: Leon Lederman, Melvin Schwartz + Jack Steinberger, 1962


Tau - τ

Mass: 1.777 GeV/c2
Charge: -1
Spin: 1/2
Generation: 3
Year and location of discovery: Martin Lewis Perl, 1975


Tau Neutrino - vτ

Mass: eV/c2
Charge: 0
Spin: 1/2
Generation: 3
Year and location of discovery: Fermilab, 2000
